I. DEFINITIONS

CLIENT is a natural or legal person who uses the investment or additional services offered by Banksters Ltd. in its capacity of investment intermediary, including when concluding transactions with securities for the own cashAccount of the intermediary.

INVESTMENT INTERMEDIARY is a local or foreign person who has the right according to its national legislation to provide professionally one or more investment services.

SECURITIES, as defined in the present rules, are transferable rights registered under accounts with a depositor institution (dematerialised securities) or documents, which materialise transferable rights (available securities), which can be offered and traded at a capital market

(1) The Investment Intermediary shall not: 1. Purchase on own Account financial instruments, for which any of its clients has submitted a purchase order and shall not sell them to clients at a price, higher than the price, at which it purchased them; 3. Sell on own or on another person's cashAccount financial instruments, which the investment intermediary or its client do not possess, except under the conditions and pursuant to Chapter 18 of the present General Terms and Conditions; 5. Participate, including as a registration agent, in concealed purchases or sales of financial instruments; 6. Receive the whole benefit, or part of it, if the investment intermediary has concluded and executed the transaction under conditions, which are more favourable than those the client has established; 7. Perform other activities in a way that threatens the interests of its clients or the stability of the financial instruments market.
