About Us

Introduction

BANK$TER$ is a licensed investment broker working with individual investors who want to trade with stocks of any public Bulgarian and international companies and who have long-term orientation using strategy "Buy & Hold". This strategy gives opportunity for receiving regular dividend income if the company-issuer of the stocks pays dividends regularly. The investors can score additional profit from the increase of the stock prices in case they decide to sell them.

Transactions

BANK$TER$ provides assistance for buying of any public Bulgarian and international companies. You can pick some of the top stocks presented on our site or you can order any other stocks by giving us the company name, stock ticker and number of stocks you want to buy. We as well assist our clients with the the stocks they want to sell. In addition we offer a competitive exchange rate for exchanging currency when international stocks are bought.

Account

We offer brokerage account which has no monthly fee and no inactivity fee. We dont have a requirement for minimal positive balance for the account. We dont have special requirement for minimal initial payment when you open your account - its your decision with what money you will start trading. There are no hidden fees - you pay only a Trade commission in the amount of $0.01 per traded stock and thus makes it more easier to predict your costs.

Consultancy

BANK$TER$ is a professional consultant who offers financial researches, economical analysis and personal advising for your overall investment strategy. We will discuss with you the most profitable stocks currently available for buying or stocks that are expected to come on the market. We will lest all the pros and cons of selling a certain stock from your portfolio. We also provide consultation about the taxation of the dividend income of our clients.
